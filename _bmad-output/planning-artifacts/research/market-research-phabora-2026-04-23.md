## Executive Summary

Phabora là nền tảng AI HR end-to-end đầu tiên tại Việt Nam với tính năng phỏng vấn video AI real-time. Nghiên cứu cho thấy có cơ hội lớn ở cả thị trường Vietnam và US, với window of opportunity 18-24 tháng trước khi cạnh tranh gay gắt.

**Key Findings:**
- Không có đối thủ nào tại Vietnam có Live AI Video Interview
- US SMB market ($49-199/mo segment) đang underserved
- Unit economics rất tốt: 92%+ gross margin, LTV/CAC ~10x
- Dual-market strategy khả thi với Vietnam = revenue, US = growth

## 5. Unit Economics

### Cost Per Operation (Phabora)

| Operation | Tokens | Cost |
|-----------|--------|------|
| Generate JD | ~600 | ~$0.002 (~50 VND) |
| Parse CV (1x, cached) | ~900 | ~$0.003 (~75 VND) |
| Score CV vs JD | ~700 | ~$0.002 (~50 VND) |
| 100 CVs scored | ~160K | ~$0.48 (~12,000 VND) |
| AI Interview (20 min) | ~3000 | ~$0.10 (~2,500 VND) |
| Knowledge Base query | ~500 | ~$0.002 (~50 VND) |

### Per-Customer Economics (Growth Tier)

```
REVENUE / CUSTOMER / MONTH
────────────────────────────────────────
Base subscription:              1,500,000 VND
Extra interviews (5 x 50K):       250,000 VND
────────────────────────────────────────
Total MRR:                      1,750,000 VND (~$70)

COGS / CUSTOMER / MONTH
────────────────────────────────────────
AI Costs:
  - 10 JDs x 50 VND:                  500 VND
  - 100 CVs parsed x 75 VND:        7,500 VND
  - 200 CV scores x 50 VND:        10,000 VND
  - 25 interviews x 2,500 VND:     62,500 VND
  - 50 KB queries x 50 VND:         2,500 VND
────────────────────────────────────────
Total AI:                          83,000 VND (~$3.30)

Infrastructure (Supabase, Vercel, LiveKit):
  - Estimated:                    ~50,000 VND (~$2)
────────────────────────────────────────
Total COGS:                       133,000 VND (~$5.30)

GROSS MARGIN
────────────────────────────────────────
Gross Profit:                   1,617,000 VND (~$64.70)
Gross Margin:                          92.4%
```

### LTV/CAC Analysis

```
ARPU:                    1,500,000 VND/month
Gross Margin:            90%
Churn Rate (monthly):    5% (assumption)
Customer Lifetime:       20 months (1/churn)

LTV = ARPU × GM × Lifetime
    = 1,500,000 × 0.9 × 20
    = 27,000,000 VND (~$1,080)

CAC = 2,500,000 VND (~$100)

LTV/CAC = 27M / 2.5M = 10.8x

→ EXCELLENT (target > 3x)
```

### Comparison with Competitors

| Platform | Price/Interview | COGS | Gross Margin |
|----------|-----------------|------|--------------|
| HireVue | $50-100 | ~$5-10 | 85-90% |
| Spark Hire | $15-25 | ~$2-3 | 85-88% |
| InterviewFlowAI | $0.99 | ~$0.30-0.50 | 50-70% |
| **Phabora** | ~$2 (50K VND) | ~$0.15 (4K VND) | **92%+** |

### Why Big Players Haven't Entered

1. **Economics Not Attractive**
   - Vietnam deal: $2K-5K/year
   - US Enterprise deal: $50K-500K/year
   - Same sales effort, 20-100x less revenue

2. **Localization Barriers**
   - Vietnamese STT/TTS quality
   - Labor law compliance
   - Payment methods (MoMo, VNPay)
   - Local sales & support team ($200K+/year)

3. **Innovator's Dilemma**
   - Going downmarket cannibalizes premium brand
   - Shareholders question margin decrease
   - Sales team not incentivized (low commission)

4. **Technical Complexity for Local Players**
   - WebRTC + STT + LLM + TTS stack
   - Need dedicated AI/ML team
   - Latency optimization difficult
   - R&D: 6-12 months, $200K-500K

## 7. Go-To-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Customers 1-10 (Month 1-3) — "Friends & Network"

**Target ICP:**
- Size: 20-100 employees
- Industry: Tech startup, Agency, BPO
- Pain: 5+ hires/month, Lead no time for interviews
- Decision maker: Founder/CEO or HR Manager

**Channels:**
1. Inner Circle (Customers 1-5)
   - Friends/ex-colleagues with startups
   - Co-working space neighbors
   - Accelerator connections
   - Pricing: FREE 3 months pilot

2. Warm Intros (Customers 6-10)
   - Referrals from customers 1-5
   - LinkedIn connections
   - Facebook groups (Startup Vietnam, HR Vietnam)
   - Pricing: 50% discount 6 months

**Outreach Template:**
```
"Chào [Tên],

Mình thấy [Company] đang tuyển [Position] trên TopCV. 

Mình đang build Phabora - platform AI interview giúp tiết kiệm 
80% thời gian phỏng vấn vòng 1. AI sẽ phỏng vấn video real-time, 
cho điểm và báo cáo chi tiết cho Lead.

Đang tìm 5 companies để pilot miễn phí 3 tháng. 
[Company] có muốn thử không?

15 phút demo call tuần này được không?"
```

### Phase 2: Customers 11-30 (Month 4-6) — "Referral Engine"

**Referral Program:**
- Referrer: 1 month FREE or 500K credit
- Referred: 1 month FREE trial + 20% off year 1

**Content Marketing:**
| Type | Platform | Frequency |

| Case study | Blog, LinkedIn | 2/month |

| Product updates | Email, Zalo | 2/month |

### Phase 3: Customers 31-50 (Month 7-9) — "Playbook Scaling"

**Outbound Motion:**
1. Build list: Scrape TopCV/VietnamWorks job postings
2. Filter: Tech/Agency/BPO, 20-200 employees, 3+ openings

**Partnership Channels:**

## 9. Risks & Mitigations

### Competitive Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| TurboHire/Zappyhire add live AI | Medium | High | Speed to market |
| Base/MISA add AI interview | Medium | High | Full lifecycle moat |
| HeyMilo enters Vietnam | Low | High | Vietnamese-first advantage |
| Local clone emerges | High | Medium | First-mover + brand |
| Enterprise players downmarket | Low | Medium | SMB pricing + local support |

### Operational Risks

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Split focus (dual market) | Medium | Clear weekly goals |
| US compliance (EEOC) | High | Legal review, documentation |
| Support across timezones | Medium | Async-first, good docs |
| AI cost spikes | Medium | Prompt optimization, caching |
| Churn in early customers | High | High-touch onboarding |

### Technical Risks

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Vietnamese STT accuracy | High | Use OpenAI Whisper (best) |
| Interview latency | Medium | Edge optimization, streaming |
| Scale issues | Medium | Load testing, architecture review |
| AI hallucinations | Medium | Structured prompts, guardrails |
